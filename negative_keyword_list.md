## ⚙️ How to Add These Lists in Google Ads

### Option A: Creating a Shared List (Recommended for Global Negatives)
1. In your Google Ads account, click the **Tools and Settings** icon (wrench) in the top menu.
2. Under the **Shared Library** column, click **Negative keyword lists**.
3. Click the blue **"+" (plus)** button.
4. Name the list **"Global Negatives (Nova Max)"**.
5. Copy the comma-separated global list above and paste it into the text box.
6. Click **Save**.
7. Click on the list you just created, select **Apply to campaigns**, and check **All Active Campaigns**.

### Option B: Adding Directly to a Campaign
1. Go to the left navigation panel and select **Campaigns** -> click on the specific campaign (e.g., *Laparoscopic & GI Surgery*).
2. Click **Keywords** in the sub-navigation panel, then select **Negative keywords** from the dropdown menu.
3. Click the blue **"+" (plus)** button.
4. Select **Add negative keywords to Campaign**.
5. Paste the respective cross-campaign negative words from Part 2.
6. Click **Save**.
